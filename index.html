The Details

  • What is Bluer & Community?

    Bluer & Community is a rolling program designed to help you on your journey towards gaining mastery of software development (and it's associated disciplines). As a member, you'll gain access to mentorship, materials, webcasts, and "living" software development projects.

  • Do I need prior programming experience to participate?

    No, we assume no knowledge and will tailor your learning path and code exposure based on both your goals and prior experience.

  • What do I need to get started?

    Literally just your computer and an Internet connection. From there we'll walk you through getting everything you need to get started.

  • How is it different to other programs?

    Research (and common sense) shows that the best way to master software development is to "learn by doing". As such, as part of the program you'll be getting your hands dirty from day one.
